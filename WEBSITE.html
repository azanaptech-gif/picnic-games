❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bright Picnic games, venues, and events

How do I download game instructions? Documents
Each game card has a green "Download Guide" button. Click it to instantly download a complete Word document containing detailed rules, equipment lists, setup diagrams, scoring systems, and tournament formats. All documents are 8-15 pages with professional illustrations. No registration required.
How does geolocation work? Contact
On the Contact page, click the "Show My Location" button. Your browser will ask for permission to access your location. Once granted, we display your precise latitude and longitude, accuracy radius in meters, and a comparison to our headquarters in Karachi. Your data is never stored or shared.
How do I join a picnic event? Events
Click the "Join the Picnic" button on the homepage. A modal window appears with complete event details: date (Sunday, 15th March 2026), time (10am-4pm), location (Green Park, Karachi), and activities (Cricket, Sack Race, Ludo, Scavenger Hunt, BBQ). Entry is free – just show up or RSVP via email.
Can I book a venue for a private event? Venues
Absolutely! Visit the Getaways page for three venue options: Green Park (BBQ pits, shelters, 200+ capacity), Beach Picnic (volleyball, swimming, lifeguard), and Hill Side (3 hiking trails, scenic views). Each listing includes distance from city, facilities, and contact information for booking.
Are there games specifically for kids? Kids
Yes! Our Ludo and Sack Race are perfect for children ages 4+. The Scavenger Hunt is designed for families with kids – we provide a 20-item list of easy-to-find natural objects. All equipment is safe and age-appropriate. Parent supervision recommended for children under 8.
Do you have games for males/females separately? Categories
While most games are co-ed, we have categorized them by preference: Chess and Carrom are popular among female players, while Cricket and Football have dedicated male tournaments. However, everyone is welcome to participate in any game! We encourage mixed teams.
Is food provided at picnic events? Catering
Yes! Our events include BBQ grill stations with vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, fresh fruit platters, homemade sandwiches, and lemonade. Dietary restrictions (halal, vegan, gluten-free) can be accommodated with 48 hours advance notice. You're also welcome to bring your own food.
What is the typical duration of a picnic event? Schedule
Our standard itinerary runs from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM (6 hours). Schedule: 10am-12pm outdoor games, 12pm-1pm lunch, 1pm-3pm indoor/family games, 3pm-4pm prize ceremony and free time. You can arrive late or leave early – it's flexible!
What happens if it rains? Weather
We have a rain contingency plan. All outdoor games move to covered picnic shelters at Green Park (6 large shelters available). Indoor games (Chess, Ludo, Carrom) continue as scheduled. In case of severe weather, we will notify registered guests 2 hours before the event and reschedule.
Can I get all game guides in one package? Downloads
Yes! Click the "Download All Guides" link in the footer. You'll receive a ZIP file containing all 7 game guides (Chess, Ludo, Carrom, Cricket, Football, Badminton, Scavenger Hunt, Sack Race) in Word format – over 60 pages of content. Perfect for event organizers.
Are the guides available in other languages? Accessibility
Currently, all documentation is in English. We are working on Urdu and Sindhi translations, expected by June 2026. For now, we provide simple visual diagrams that are easy to follow regardless of language. Email us if you need special assistance.
How can I provide feedback or suggestions? Feedback
We love hearing from you! Visit the Contact page and fill out the feedback form. Select a category (Indoor, Outdoor, Family, Itinerary, Technical, or General), enter your name and email, and write your message. We typically respond within 24 hours. You can also email us directly at hello@brightpicnic.com.
